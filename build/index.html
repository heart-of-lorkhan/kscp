Could you be a best friend?

Being a Care Assistant at Kent Social Care Professionals is more than providing personal care and home visits — it’s about building connections and becoming a best friend.

Join the Kent SCP team

At Kent SCP, our dedicated carers step out into the local community every day to provide quality care service to elderly or vulnerable customers within the comfort of their own homes. Our Care Assistants enjoy a rewarding career where they make a positive difference to someone’s life, providing independence, support and a best friend to share their daily activities with. As a Care Assistant with Kent SCP you will be supported every step of the way whilst you bring hope to those who need it most.

Role and responsibilities

We support people over the age of 18 with person-centred care, such as physical disabilities, dementia, mental health needs, learning difficulties, elderly frail and end of life care. Other responsibilities include:

  • Medication support
  • Nutritional and fluids support & monitoring
  • Shopping
  • Social support, such as attending the cinema, beach, and other days out
  • Incontinence, stoma and catheter care
  • Working with other stakeholders such as district nurses
  • Palliative care
  • Domestic support
  • Mental health support
  • Supporting families and mediating when they face challenges

Our mission and values

Our core aim is to deliver flexible, community-based care support in Kent, always striving to promote independence, dignity and choice among clients.

  • We Listen

  • We Build
